The Happy Prince

                                                        The happy prince was a statue in a city.His body was gilded                                                  with golden leaves,his eyes were sapphires. He had a red ruby on                                                   gilt of his sword.

                                                   One night, a little Swallow flew down between the feet of the                                                  Happy Prince . The Happy Prince asked it to take his ruby to a                                         seamstress whose boy was ill.

                                                               The Happy Prince asked it to pluck his sapphire eye and                                                                 give it to hungry man.The following night the Happy                                                                       prince told give other to a little sad match-girl.

                                                                     The Happy prince was now blind. The Happy Prince                                                         told the swallow to remove its gold leaves and give it to poor.

                                                                  The snow came . The little Swallow grew cold.It Fell                                                                dead at his feet. The Lead heart of the statue broke.

Next morning,the statue was melted in the furnace. The workmen found that the broken lead heart would not melt.They threw it away in the heap where the dead swallow lay.
