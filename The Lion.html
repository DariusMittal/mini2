The Lion

Lions live in grasslands,scrubs and open woodlands of Africa. It is the second Largest Cat in the
world.It is smaller than the Tiger,which is closely related .

Unlike other cats,lions are very social animals.They                 live in groups,called prides,of around 30 lions.Pride members keep track of one                                 another by roaring.Both males and females have a very powerful roar.

Male lion has a strong body.Its eyes have a                                               glowing look.It has Sharp teeth and strong claws.Its body is covered                                           with greyish small and smooth hair.Its paws are very strong.

Male Lions spend their time guarding their                                                 territory an their cubs.They Maintain the boundaries of their                                               territory . Their thick manes ,a unique trait to male lions , protect their                                               necks when they fight with challengers.

Female lions are the hunters of the.They are smaller                                               and more agile than males .They use teamwork to bring an                                                 animal down. Their prey includes antelopes,buffaloes,zebras,young                                               elephants,rhinos,hippos,wild hogs,crocodiles and                                                    giraffes.But they also sometimes eat smaller prey like mice,birds                                             ,hares,lizards,and tortoises.

Lions are the laziest of the big cats.They can be
found lying on their backs with their feet up
or taking a snooze up in a tree.
